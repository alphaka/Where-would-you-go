WHERE/WOULD/YOU/GO?

Even though I am currently living in one of my cities of choice, NEW YORK, I still love exploring The Big Apple and wouldn't hesitate to visit any of the following places.

NEW YORK, NY

In New York, you'll find The Met and the Guggenheim Museum, which are some of the world's most famous art museums. As well, you'll discover this phenomenal architectural work on the left/below, called The Vessel.

LONDON, UK

The United Kingdom's capital, London, is renowned for its status as one of the world's greatest cosmopolitan cities. With an incredible history regarding its royalty, politics, arts, science, and marvelous architecture.

LOS ANGELES, CA

Besides its fabulous weather throughout the year, as a movie fan, I couldn't imagine making this list without highlighting the heart of my favorites film source, Hollywood. Hollywood is the flesh and bone of the American film Industry.

ABU DHABI, AE

Home to one of the most elegant mosques on earth, The Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque is an architectural piece of art in Abu Dhabi that I cherish visiting. The city is also known for its luxurious racing circuit, and some of the most famous races in the world have taken place here.

CAPE TOWN, ZA

Cape Town's magnificent weather year-round makes it a popular destination for beach lovers like me. Cape Town's world-famous Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens and Green Point Park complements its natural blessings wonderfully.

FUJIYOSHIDA, JP

Undoubtedly, Fujiyoshida is famous for its epic Mount Fuji, Japan's most sacred symbol. Fujiyoshida is also the hometown of the attractive Fuji Sengen Shrine and The Fuji-Q Highland theme park.
